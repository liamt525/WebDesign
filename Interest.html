Ski Mountain Reviews

By: Liam Tomaszewski

Welcome to Ski Mountain Reviews by Liam Tomaszewski. On this site you will find reviews for various mountains in the Northeast and out west in the Rockies. When I started skiing, I wanted to make sure that I was going to the right mountains for my level. I want you to have the same experience. What are you waiting for? Go get some skis and I'll catch you on the slopes By the way, lodging means hotels not the the actual lodge. Don't get intimidated by any ski mountain. All of them are great and I recommend ski school for any beginner skier or anyone who wants a lesson. I will be updating this site time to time to add new places I have been to!!!
